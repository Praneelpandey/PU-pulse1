# 🎓 PU Pulse | Campus Essentials

[![Deployed on Vercel](https://img.shields.io/badge/Deployed%20on-Vercel-black?logo=vercel)](https://pu-pulse-3.vercel.app/)
[![License: MIT](https://img.shields.io/badge/License-MIT-yellow.svg)](https://opensource.org/licenses/MIT)

**PU Pulse** is a centralized, all-in-one web platform designed to keep students connected with the heartbeat of the campus. It provides students, faculty, and staff with essential updates, academic resources, event tracking, and campus news in a single, intuitive dashboard.

🌍 **Live Demo:** [https://pu-pulse-3.vercel.app/](https://pu-pulse-3.vercel.app/)

---

## ✨ Features

* **📰 Campus News & Announcements:** Real-time updates on university notices, deadlines, and breaking campus news.
* **📅 Event Pulse:** Discover and track upcoming college fests, workshops, seminars, and club activities.
* **📚 Academic Essentials:** Quick access to syllabus trackers, previous year question papers, and study materials.
* **🗺️ Campus Directory:** Important contact information, club details, and campus maps.
* **📱 Responsive Design:** A mobile-first UI ensuring a seamless experience across desktop, tablet, and mobile devices.
